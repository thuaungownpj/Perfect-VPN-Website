Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 23, 2026

Introduction
Welcome to Perfect VPN. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using Perfect VPN, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Strict No-Logs Policy

Perfect VPN is built on a strict No-Logs policy. We DO NOT collect, store, or share any of the following data:

  • Your browsing history or visited websites.
  • Your downloaded or uploaded files.
  • Your originating IP address or DNS queries.
  • Your VPN network traffic and connection timestamps.

We ensure that your online activities remain completely private and anonymous.

2. Core VpnService Usage

Our app utilizes Android's VpnService API as its core functionality to create a secure, encrypted, and device-level virtual private network. This service is used exclusively to route your internet traffic securely through our servers to protect your privacy and provide a stable connection. No traffic data is monitored, recorded, or shared during this process.

3. Data We Collect and How We Use It

To maintain and improve our app, we only collect the absolute minimum necessary data:

  • App Performance & Crash Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics to collect anonymous crash reports and basic app usage statistics. This helps us fix bugs and improve performance. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected here.
  • Advertising Data: As a completely free service, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID to serve personalized ads.
  • Installed Apps (Split Tunneling): To provide the "Split Tunneling" feature, our app requests permission to view your installed applications. This allows you to choose which specific apps bypass the VPN. This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Camera Permission: We request camera access exclusively for scanning QR codes to add custom V2Ray server configurations. Images are processed locally and are never stored, recorded, or uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, strictly governed by their respective Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services (For app distribution)
  • Google AdMob (For advertising)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase / Crashlytics (For app analytics and crash reporting)

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures and modern encryption protocols (V2Ray) to protect your connection. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Perfect VPN does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete it from our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
