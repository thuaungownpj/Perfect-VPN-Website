About Perfect VPN

Welcome to Perfect VPN! We believe that a secure, private, and unrestricted internet is a fundamental right for everyone.

Our mission is to provide top-tier online security and digital freedom without the premium price tag. Perfect VPN is a completely free, fast, and highly reliable virtual private network built on the advanced V2Ray protocol, designed to keep your digital life safe and anonymous.

Why Choose Perfect VPN?

  • 100% Free: We are committed to keeping our core services completely free for all users, supported by non-intrusive advertisements.
  • Advanced Security: Utilizing the robust V2Ray protocol to ensure your data remains deeply encrypted and safe from hackers or trackers on any network.
  • Strict No-Logs Policy: Your privacy is our absolute priority. We do not track, record, store, or share your browsing history or online activities.
  • Split Tunneling: Giving you full control to choose exactly which applications use the VPN connection and which use your regular local network.
  • Custom Configurations: Enjoy the flexibility to easily add your own custom servers using QR code scanning or manual setups.

Our Commitment

Our dedicated development team is constantly working behind the scenes to maintain a highly stable server network and continuously improve the app's performance. We are here to bring you a seamless, secure, and user-friendly experience.

Thank you for trusting Perfect VPN as your secure gateway to the internet.
